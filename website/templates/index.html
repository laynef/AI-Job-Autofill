Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Hired Always

Is Hired Always really free?

Yes! Hired Always is completely free forever. Get unlimited job application autofills at no cost. No credit card required, no hidden fees, no subscriptions. Just install the extension and start using it immediately.

Which job platforms are supported?

Hired Always works with the most popular job application systems including Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, Ashby, BambooHR, Gem, Jobvite, and SmartRecruiters. We're constantly adding support for more platforms.

Is my data safe and private?

Yes! Your resume and personal information are stored securely. We don't sell your data to third parties. Usage tracking is anonymized for improving the service. You have complete control over your information.

Do I need technical skills to use this?

Not at all! Hired Always is designed to be simple. Just install the Chrome extension, fill out your profile once, and click the "Autofill" button when you're on a job application page. It takes less than 3 minutes to get started.

How does the AI cover letter work?

Our AI analyzes the job description and your resume to create a custom cover letter for each position. You can review and edit it before submitting to ensure it matches your voice and highlights the most relevant experience.

Are there any usage limits?

No! Hired Always is completely free with unlimited usage. Apply to as many jobs as you want without any restrictions or paywalls.

What if the autofill doesn't work on a site?

While we support the most popular platforms, some custom application systems may not be compatible. You can still manually fill those forms. We're always working to add support for more sites based on user feedback.

How do you make money if it's free?

Hired Always is a passion project to help job seekers save time and land their dream jobs. The service is completely free with no ads or hidden costs.
